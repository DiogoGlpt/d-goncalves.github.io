Slizzle -Ongoing-

Story

Slie, a slime that was created by a mad scientist that gained consciousness and intelligence, after a failed experiment. The science lab in which Slie was created was mainly used to conduct dangerous and lethal experiments on slimes. After hearing the scientists talking about the next experiment, about to be tested on him, the slime desperately tries to escape the lab. What Slie doesnâ€™t know is that this lab is a very secure facility, and he would have a hard time trying to get out of it.

Gameplay

The player must execute various actions to solve semi-complex puzzles in order to complete a level. Each level has multiple ways of completion to encourage replayability. The puzzles consist of opening doors by triggering, for example, levers, and moving boxes onto pressure plates. There are also several movement mechanics, such as walls made out of slime where the player can stick to and jump off of, or pipes that the player can go through to reach places they otherwise cannot.

World

The world is set in a foreseeable future where an organization has started to experiment with a certain substance to create slimes. The environment depicts this, as the playable world is within a Science Lab. Players can find notes scattered throughout the levels to discover more about the world's story.
